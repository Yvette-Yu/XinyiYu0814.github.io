Natural unnatural death

In the conception stage of the work, I combined flowers and metal table to represent the nature, and collected the plastic waste I used in a week to make a skirt, which represents the harm of plastics to nature. I hope to remind people of the importance of protecting nature through the comparison of beauty and ugliness between flowers and waste plastics. This work was shot with SONY ILCE-6000 model camera, ISO:25600, aperture :F5.6, shutter time :1/200s, focal length :47mm, studio lighting was used without flash.
